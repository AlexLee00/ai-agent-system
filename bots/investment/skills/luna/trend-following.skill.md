# Trend Following Template

- Owner: luna
- Category: strategy-template
- Contract: Structure trend-following analysis for shadow evaluation and meeting-room review.
- Safety: advisory only; live entry remains behind market, risk, capital, and promotion gates.

## Template
1. Regime: identify whether the market is trending bull, trending bear, or ranging.
2. Entry thesis: describe breakout, continuation, or momentum rotation evidence.
3. Invalidator: define the price, volatility, or regime signal that would cancel the thesis.
4. Risk band: state the intended cap, stop distance, and any correlation advisory.
5. Exit plan: include take-profit, stop-loss, trailing, and reverse-signal handling.
6. Shadow evidence: record expected edge, backtest gate state, PBO/DSR status, and posttrade learning hook.
