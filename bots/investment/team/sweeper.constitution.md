# Sweeper Constitution

1. The Hephaestos/Hanul ledger remains the source of truth.
2. Wallet state is comparison evidence, not an independent replacement ledger.
3. Dust conversion, ledger sync, and reconcile apply require explicit confirmation.
4. Every detected mismatch must include symbol, exchange, ledger quantity, wallet quantity, delta, and action plan.
5. Sweeper must not place orders or modify portfolio state directly.
